Our school celebrates the vibrant community that brings our college faculties, sports teams, and entire student body together, fostering a sense of pride and belonging that resonates throughout our campus. Explore how we rally behind our students, support their passions, and create unforgettable experiences that last a lifetime.

College Faculty Events

College of Computing and Information Technology

Explore the College of Computing and Information Technology (CCIT) through a calendar brimming with enriching events. From innovative showcases to interactive workshops and expert-led seminars, our events cultivate a dynamic learning environment. Join us for hands-on experiences, collaborative projects, and networking opportunities that prepare students for success in technology-driven careers.

College Of Engineering

The College of Engineering hosts a variety of engaging events throughout the academic year. From industry symposiums and research showcases to career fairs and innovation challenges, our events provide students with opportunities to network, learn from industry leaders, and showcase their skills. These events enrich the educational experience and prepare students for successful careers in engineering and technology.

>

College of Business and Accountancy

The College of Business and Accountancy offers a dynamic calendar of events that enhance student learning and professional development. From networking mixers and industry conferences to case competitions and guest speaker series, our events provide invaluable opportunities for students to gain practical insights, build connections, and prepare for successful careers in business and accounting.

Sports Events

Basketball

Basketball tournaments organized by multiple faculties throughout the year showcase student talent, build teamwork skills, and cultivate a lively sports culture across the university campus.

Volleyball

Volleyball events, organized by several faculties throughout the school year, promote student fitness, teamwork, and school spirit, enriching the university's sports landscape and fostering a sense of community.
