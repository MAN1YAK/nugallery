Common Area, Arts & Cultural Facilities and Gym

Mariano Jhocson Monument

This statue of Don Mariano Fortunato Jhocson commemorates his dedication to instilling competence, leadership, and high moral values in the youth through education guided by the philosophy of Dynamic Filipinism. Explore this tribute to the man whose legacy continues to inspire our vibrant academic community.

Accounting and Registrar Lobby

Our Accounting and Registrar Lobby exemplifies the efficiency and support within our campus. Experience a space dedicated to seamless financial and academic services, where modern facilities meet expert assistance.

Jhocson Memorial Wall

The Wall of Awardees, where the Don Mariano Fortunato Jhocson Memorial Award shines as National University's highest honor. Each plaque celebrates graduates who have excelled academically, demonstrated exemplary leadership, and actively contributed to our community through diverse activities. This gallery showcases their outstanding character, moral strength, and commitment to excellence, inspiring all who visit to emulate their achievements. It's a testament to the vibrant spirit and legacy of National University, celebrating the achievements that define our university community.

School Court/Gym

Our gymnasium is a vibrant center for athletic excellence and school spirit. Explore our top-tier facilities, featuring a spacious basketball court, modern equipment, and seating areas for spectators. These snapshots capture the dynamic environment where our students train, compete, and achieve their best.

Classrooms, Lecture Rooms and Computer Lab

Lecture Room

Welcome to our lecture room, designed for interactive learning experiences. Students engage with multimedia content and participate actively in dynamic educational sessions. This space is tailored for effective teaching and collaboration, integrating technology to enhance the learning process.

Computer Lab

Welcome to our Computer Lab, where students are supported in coursework, research, and digital projects like coding and simulations. Expert assistance ensures a seamless experience, empowering students to develop essential skills effectively. Welcome to a space where technology meets education, fostering innovation and practical learning.

Nursing Labaratory

Welcome to our Nursing Laboratory, a dedicated space for hands-on training and skill development. Equipped with advanced medical equipment and simulation technology, students gain practical experience in a controlled environment. Expert guidance ensures comprehensive learning, preparing students for real-world healthcare scenarios with confidence.

HRM Hot Kitchen

Welcome to the Hot Kitchen, where culinary and HRM students hone their skills in a professional setting. Equipped with modern appliances and tools, this lab fosters hands-on learning in food preparation, cooking techniques, and kitchen management. Students gain practical experience under expert guidance, preparing them for careers in the culinary industry.

Engineering Labaratory

Welcome to our Engineering Laboratory, where innovation meets hands-on learning. Equipped with cutting-edge technology and specialized equipment, students engage in practical experiments and projects across various disciplines. Expert guidance ensures a robust educational experience, preparing students for challenges in engineering and technological advancement.
